Privacy Policy

Divvy

1. Basic Policy

"Divvy" (hereinafter "the App") respects user privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.

Halu Studio (hereinafter "we" or "us") handles user information of the App as follows.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally by the App

  • Bill splitting calculation history (amounts, number of people, notes, etc.)
  • App settings
  • Ad removal purchase information

All of this information is stored only on your device and is never sent to external servers.

2.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect information independently:

  • Google AdMob (Ad delivery)
  • Apple StoreKit (In-app purchases)

3. Purpose of Use

Information is used to provide bill splitting features, save calculation history, maintain app settings, and manage ad removal purchase status.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, share, or provide users' calculation data or personal information to third parties, except when users use the "Share Results" feature or when disclosure is required by law.

5. ATT (App Tracking Transparency)

The App uses the ATT framework to request tracking permission for delivering personalized ads on iOS 14.5 and later.

6. Camera and Photo Library Access

The App requests access to the camera and photo library for the receipt scanning feature. Captured/selected images are used only for OCR processing and are not sent externally or saved on the device.

7. Data Retention Period

  • Calculation history: Until deleted by user or app uninstallation
  • App settings: Until app uninstallation
  • Purchase information: Permanently stored on Apple's servers

8. Data Deletion

Users can delete calculation history individually from the "History" tab, or delete all data by uninstalling the app.

9. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in response to legal changes or service modifications. If there are significant changes, we will notify users within the app.
