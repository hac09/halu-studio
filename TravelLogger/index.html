Privacy Policy

TravelLogger

1. Basic Policy

Halu Studio (hereinafter "we") respects the privacy of users of this application "TravelLogger" (hereinafter "this app") and endeavors to protect personal information, handling it as follows.

2. Information We Collect

This app collects the following information:

2.1 Location Information

  • GPS location data (latitude, longitude, altitude)
  • Including background location tracking
  • Location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers

2.2 Photo Information

  • Photos selected by the user
  • Photo metadata (date/time taken, location information)
  • Photos are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to external servers

2.3 Information Required for Ad Delivery

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage statistics

2.4 App Usage Information

  • Number of recorded logs
  • App launch count
  • Crash reports (for bug fixes)

3. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

3.1 Recording Travel Routes

  • GPS location data is used to record your travel routes
  • Recorded data is stored only on your device

3.2 Recording Photos

  • Selected photos are associated with travel routes and recorded
  • Photo data is stored only on your device

3.3 Ad Display

  • The free version displays ads using Google AdMob
  • Advertising identifiers and device information are used for ad delivery

3.4 Service Improvement

  • App usage and crash reports are analyzed to improve the service
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services for ad delivery:

4.1 Google AdMob

  • Used for ad delivery
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

These services collect and use information based on their own privacy policies. Please refer to each service's privacy policy for details. We do not provide information to any third parties other than those mentioned above.

5. Data Management

5.1 Local Storage

  • Location data, photos, and log data are stored only on your device
  • No cloud sync or transmission to external servers
  • Your data belongs only to you

5.2 Data Deletion

  • Deleting the app will permanently delete all log data and photos
  • Recovery is not possible, so please be careful

5.3 Security

  • Collected information is managed with appropriate security measures
  • Reasonable measures are implemented against risks such as unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, and leakage

6. About Pro Version

When you purchase the Pro version, ads will be hidden. There are no other changes to the privacy policy.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this privacy policy as necessary. The updated privacy policy will take effect when posted within the app.
